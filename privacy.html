Privacy Policy – EchoMind

EchoMind respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

EchoMind processes voice recordings and transcripts to generate summaries, key points, and action items. Audio recordings may be temporarily transmitted to AI services for transcription and analysis.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely to provide AI-powered note generation, voice transcription, and productivity features within the app.

Data Storage

Voice recordings and generated notes are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them. Users can delete recordings and notes at any time within the app.

Third-Party Services

EchoMind may use third-party services including AI processing providers and subscription management services to operate the app.

Subscriptions

EchoMind offers optional subscriptions that unlock additional features such as unlimited recordings and advanced export options.
