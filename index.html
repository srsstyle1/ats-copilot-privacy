Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

ATS Copilot ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

ATS Copilot does not require account creation. We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The app processes resume text and job descriptions solely to provide AI-powered analysis.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Resume and job description content is securely transmitted to a third-party AI service provider (OpenAI) for processing. Data is used only to generate analysis results and is not stored on our servers.

3. Data Storage

  • Resume files are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not maintain a database of user resumes.
  • We do not sell or share user data.

4. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple In-App Purchases. We do not access payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI (AI processing)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Apple In-App Purchases

6. Data Security

All server communication is secured via HTTPS encryption.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
